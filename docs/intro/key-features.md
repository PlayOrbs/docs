# Key Features

PlayOrbs combines competitive gameplay with sustainable economics through several innovative features.

## Tiered Competition

Multiple entry tiers cater to different risk appetites. Higher tiers offer:
- Larger prize pools
- Higher points multipliers
- More competitive opponents

Check the game interface for current tier configurations.

## Seasonal Leaderboards

The game operates in seasons:
- **60,000 rounds per season** (~3 months)
- Points accumulate throughout the season
- Top performers share the season pool
- Rankings reset each season for fresh competition

## Token Emissions

PORB tokens are earned, not pre-allocated:

| Recipient | Amount per Emission |
|-----------|---------------------|
| 1st Joiner | 100 PORB |
| 2nd Joiner | 60 PORB |
| 3rd Joiner | 40 PORB |
| Season Pool | 50 PORB |

**Emission probability:**
- Increases linearly since last emission
- Guaranteed by round 20
- Average: ~1 emission per 10 rounds

## Early Exit (EE)

An optional early exit mechanism:
- Set a target earnings when joining
- Automatically exit if target is hit
- Reduces risk but may limit maximum gains
- Available in select tiers

## Referral Program

Invite friends and earn:
- **10%** of referred players' entry fees (SOL)
- **20 points** per referred player join
- Milestone bonuses at 50 and 250 referrals

## Achievement System

Earn bonus points for reaching milestones:

### Seasonal Achievements (reset each season)
- First Blood - Play 1 round
- Getting Warm - Play 5 rounds
- Committed - Play 25 rounds
- Champion - Win 1 round
- Killing Spree - 3+ kills in one round
- Hot Streak - 2 consecutive wins

### Lifetime Achievements (one-time)
- Nickname Pioneer - First to set nickname
- Referrer Pro - Refer 50 players
- Referrer Legend - Refer 250 players

Check the app for current point values.

## Fair Launch Economics

Unlike traditional P2E games:
- **No pre-mine**: Zero tokens allocated to team
- **No VC rounds**: No investor allocations
- **Earned only**: 100% of tokens come from gameplay
- **Liquidity-backed**: Every PORB backed by real game fees

## Provably Fair Randomness

Settlement uses cryptographic verification:
- Seeds from ICP's threshold cryptography
- Merkle proofs bind seeds to rounds
- Anyone can verify fairness independently
