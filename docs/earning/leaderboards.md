# Leaderboards

Leaderboards track player performance throughout each season.

## How Rankings Work

Players are ranked by **season points**:

1. Every round you play earns points
2. Points accumulate throughout the season
3. Higher points = higher leaderboard position
4. Top performers earn larger season pool shares

## Leaderboard Types

### Season Leaderboard
- Resets each season
- Determines pool share allocation
- Fresh competition every ~3 months

## Climbing the Leaderboard

### Points Multipliers

Higher tiers earn points faster:

| Tier | Multiplier | Climb Speed |
|------|------------|-------------|
| 0 | 1.0x | Normal |
| 1 | 1.5x | 50% faster |
| 2 | 2.0x | 100% faster |
| 3 | 3.0x | 200% faster |

### Performance Matters

Points breakdown for a typical win:

| Component | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Participation | 50 |
| 1st Place | 500 |
| 3 Kills | 300 |
| **Base Total** | **850** |
| Tier 2 (2.0x) | **1,700** |

### Consistency Wins

Regular play compounds:

| Games/Day | Daily Points* | Weekly | Monthly |
|-----------|---------------|--------|---------|
| 5 | 1,000 | 7,000 | 30,000 |
| 10 | 2,000 | 14,000 | 60,000 |
| 20 | 4,000 | 28,000 | 120,000 |

*Assuming average performance at Tier 1*

## Season Pool Share

Your leaderboard position determines your pool share. The more season points you accumulate relative to other players, the larger your share of the season pool rewards.

## Leaderboard Strategies

### Volume Strategy
- Play many games at lower tiers
- Consistent small point gains
- Lower risk, steady climb

### High-Tier Strategy
- Focus on higher tiers
- Fewer games but bigger points per game
- Higher variance but faster potential climb

### Hybrid Strategy
- Mix tiers based on confidence
- Scale up during winning streaks
- Step down to preserve gains

## Tracking Your Progress

Monitor these stats:

| Metric | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Season Points | Current season standing |
| Rank | Position vs other players |
| Games Played | Activity level |
| Win Rate | Performance indicator |
| Points/Game | Efficiency metric |

## Season End

When the season ends:

1. Final leaderboard is frozen
2. Season snapshot created
3. Claims open for pool distribution
4. New season starts automatically
