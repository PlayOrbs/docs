# Achievements

Achievements reward you with bonus points for reaching gameplay milestones.

## Achievement Types

### Seasonal Achievements
Reset each season, giving everyone fresh goals:

| Achievement | Requirement |
|-------------|-------------|
| First Blood | Play 1 round |
| Getting Warm | Play 5 rounds |
| Committed | Play 25 rounds |
| Miner | Earn PPORB emission |
| Champion | Win 1 round |
| Killing Spree | 3+ kills in one round |
| Hot Streak | 2 consecutive wins |

### Lifetime Achievements
Unlock once and keep forever:

| Achievement | Requirement |
|-------------|-------------|
| Nickname Pioneer | First to set nickname |
| Referrer Rookie | Refer 1 player |
| Referrer Pro | Refer 50 players |
| Referrer Legend | Refer 250 players |

Check the app for current bonus point values.

## Achievement Categories

### Participation
Rewards consistent play:
- First Blood (1 round)
- Getting Warm (5 rounds)
- Committed (25 rounds)

### Performance
Rewards skill:
- Champion (1 win)
- Killing Spree (3+ kills)
- Hot Streak (consecutive wins)

### Earning
Rewards token acquisition:
- Miner (receive PORB emission)

### Social
Rewards community building:
- Referrer tiers (1/50/250 referrals)
- Nickname Pioneer (first to claim)

## Tracking Progress

Your dashboard shows:
- Completed achievements
- Progress toward next achievements
- Points earned from achievements

## Strategy Tips

### Early Season
Focus on:
1. First Blood (just play once)
2. Getting Warm (play 5 rounds)
3. Champion (win one round)

### Mid Season
Work toward:
1. Committed (25 rounds)
2. Killing Spree (aggressive play)
3. Hot Streak (consecutive wins)

### Ongoing
Always try for:
1. Miner (be first 3 joiners)
2. Referrals (build your network)

## Achievement Notifications

When you unlock an achievement:
- On-screen notification appears
- Points credited immediately
- Achievement logged to your profile

## Resetting Mechanics

### Seasonal Reset
At season end:
- Seasonal achievements reset
- Can re-earn same achievements
- Lifetime achievements persist

### No Double Counting
- Each achievement can only be earned once per period
- Re-completing requirements doesn't grant additional points
