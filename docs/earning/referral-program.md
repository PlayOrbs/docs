# Referral Program

Invite friends to PlayOrbs and earn a share of their entry fees.

## How It Works

1. **Get your referral link** from your dashboard
2. **Share it** with friends
3. **Earn rewards** when they play

## Referral Rewards

When a referred player joins a round, you earn:

| Reward Type | Amount |
|-------------|--------|
| SOL | 10% of their entry fee |
| Points | 20 points per join |

### Example Earnings

If your referral joins Tier 2 (1 SOL entry):

```
Entry fee: 1 SOL
Protocol share: 0.2 SOL (20%)
Your referral reward: 0.002 SOL (10% of dev portion)
```

## Reward Flow

```
Referred Player Entry
    │
    └── 80% → Prize Pool
    └── 20% → Protocol
            │
            ├── 50% → LP Vault
            │
            └── 50% → Dev Split
                    │
                    ├── 90% → Dev Wallet
                    │
                    └── 10% → Referrer (You!)
```

## Setting Up Referrals

### Creating Your Link

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Get Referral Link"
3. Copy and share your unique URL

## Milestone Bonuses

Earn bonus points for referral milestones:

| Milestone | Referrals | Bonus Points |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Referrer Rookie | 1 | 500 |
| Referrer Pro | 50 | 5,000 |
| Referrer Legend | 250 | 10,000 |

## Claiming Rewards

Referral rewards accumulate in your vault:

1. Go to your dashboard
2. View "Referral Earnings"
3. Click "Claim" to withdraw

## Tips for Referrers

### Maximize Earnings

- Target active players who'll play regularly
- Focus on players interested in higher tiers
- Share your link in gaming communities

### Track Your Network

Monitor:
- Total referrals
- Active referrals (played in last 7 days)
- Total SOL earned
- Points from referral bonuses

## Referral Rules

### One Referrer Per Player
- Referrer is set permanently on first game
- Cannot be changed later

### Self-Referral Prevention
- You cannot refer yourself
- Circular referrals are blocked

### Reward Calculation
- Rewards calculated per transaction
- Paid from protocol fees (not player pools)
