# Getting Started

This guide walks you through connecting your wallet and playing your first PlayOrbs round.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

1. **Solana Wallet**: Phantom, Solflare, or any Solana-compatible wallet
2. **SOL Balance**: Enough to cover entry fees + transaction costs (~0.001 SOL per tx)
3. **Game Client**: Access via the official PlayOrbs interface

## Connecting Your Wallet

1. Navigate to the PlayOrbs platform
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection

Your wallet address becomes your player identity throughout the game.

## Your First Round

### Step 1: Choose a Tier

Select a tier based on your risk tolerance and SOL balance. Lower tiers are recommended for new players while higher tiers offer bigger rewards for more competitive play. Check the game interface for current tier configurations.

### Step 2: Join a Round

1. Click **"Join"** on your chosen tier
2. Review the entry fee and round details
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. Wait for the transaction to complete

### Step 3: Wait for Countdown

Once minimum players join:
- The countdown timer starts
- Additional players can still join
- Round begins when timer expires or max players reached

### Step 4: Compete

During the battle royale:
- Your orb spawns in the arena
- Collide with opponents to eliminate them
- Break tethers to score kills
- Survive as long as possible

### Step 5: Collect Rewards

After settlement:
- **Winners** receive SOL prizes automatically
- **All players** earn points based on performance
- **First 3 joiners** may receive PORB emissions

## Understanding Your Dashboard

After connecting, you'll see:

- **Balance**: Your SOL and PORB holdings
- **Stats**: Rounds played, wins, kills
- **Season Points**: Current season accumulation
- **Leaderboard Position**: Your ranking

## Tips for New Players

1. **Start with Tier 0**: Lower entry while learning
2. **Watch the countdown**: More players = bigger prize pool
3. **Check emission status**: Being first to join has advantages
4. **Track your points**: They accumulate for season rewards
5. **Set a referral link**: Earn from players you invite
