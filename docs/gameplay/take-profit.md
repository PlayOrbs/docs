# Early Exit (Legacy)

:::caution Removed Feature
Early Exit (EE) was part of the v1 rewards system. In the current rewards system, **EE is disabled** — all players compete until elimination or victory. This page is kept for historical reference.
:::

## How It Worked (v1)

Early Exit let players set a target earnings amount before the round. If their bounty earnings hit the target during gameplay, they automatically claimed and exited — their orb was removed from the arena.

### EE Presets (v1)

| Preset | Target | Strategy |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Safe | ~2 kills value | Minimize risk |
| Balanced | ~4 kills value | Moderate approach |
| Aggressive | ~6 kills value | High upside |
| YOLO | No EE | Maximum risk/reward |

## Why It Was Removed

The new rewards system replaced EE with a model where survival itself is rewarded:

- **Top 3 share survival pot**: With the survival pot (60% of player pool) now split among top 3 finishers (65/25/10), there's a strong incentive to stay and compete for placement
- **Simpler gameplay**: No need to set targets or manage exit strategy before a round
- **Fairer outcomes**: Every player competes under the same rules — no early exits disrupting the lobby

## Current Model

In the current system:
- All players compete until eliminated or last standing
- Kill bounties are earned per elimination via the [log-weighted schedule](/economics/kill-rewards)
- Top 3 finishers share the [survival pot](/economics/payout-mechanics)
- Even 2nd and 3rd place receive meaningful rewards
